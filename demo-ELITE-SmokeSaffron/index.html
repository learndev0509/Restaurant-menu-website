Welcome to Smoke & Saffron

Cooked over live fire

A wood-fire kitchen where the tandoor is lit fresh each day and the bar keeps pouring long after the city sleeps.

Slow-simmered makhanis, char-finished tandoor, hand-stretched wood-fired pizza, and cocktails till midnight. Come for a quiet dinner, a long celebration, or a late-night table with friends — the fire's always on.
