Welcome to The White Root

A family kitchen, reimagined

From a small family kitchen in Chandkheda to one of Ahmedabad's most-loved dining destinations — every dish carries the heart of home.

Slow-simmered makhanis, char-finished tandoor, wood-fired pizzas, and a coffee bar that pours till midnight. Whether you're here for a quiet evening, a celebration, or a Sunday feast — there's a flavour for you.
